# App Store privacy answers

Proposed answers, subject to verification against the final binary and App Store Connect taxonomy:

- Data used to track you: **No**
- Data linked to you and collected by the developer: **No**
- Third-party analytics: **No**
- Third-party advertising: **No**
- Developer relay/backend: **No**
- Account creation in Codex Meter: **No**
- User content uploaded to developer servers: **No**

Authentication and usage data are exchanged directly between the user's device and OpenAI at the user's request. Tokens stay in Keychain; cached usage stays on device. Confirm whether Apple expects these direct third-party-service exchanges to be declared under “Other Data” for the final questionnaire.
