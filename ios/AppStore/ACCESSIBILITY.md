# Accessibility statement and checks

Codex Meter uses semantic SwiftUI controls, Dynamic Type, VoiceOver labels for meters and actions, system contrast colors, Reduce Motion-aware system transitions, and layouts that stack at accessibility sizes.

Before submission, verify on device:

- VoiceOver reading order and action labels for onboarding, dashboard, Settings, reset confirmation, and Live Activity controls.
- All accessibility text sizes in portrait and landscape without clipped primary actions.
- Increase Contrast, Differentiate Without Color, Reduce Transparency, and Reduce Motion.
- Apple Watch 40/42/46 mm and Ultra with VoiceOver and largest text.
