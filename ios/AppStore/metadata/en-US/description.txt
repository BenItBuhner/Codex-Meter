Codex Meter is an unofficial, open-source viewer for the Codex allowance attached to your existing ChatGPT account.

At a glance:
• Five-hour and weekly allowance meters
• Reset timers and reset-credit inventory
• Usage-pace projections with adjustable warnings
• Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets
• Optional Live Activity for Lock Screen and Dynamic Island
• Apple Watch dashboard, Smart Stack widgets, and complications
• Local notifications for usage, resets, refills, and credit expiry
• Offline cache and a network-free demo mode

Privacy by design:
• OAuth credentials stay in Apple Keychain
• Requests go directly from your device to OpenAI
• Apple Watch and widgets receive sanitized usage only
• Settings exports never include credentials, identity, or cached usage
• No analytics, advertising, or relay server
